Domestic Tours in India

GOA - The Land Of Sun & Sand

Goa is a beautiful paradise where blue water, silvery sand, and long coconut trees conceal the natural beauty of the city. Apart from the rich legacy of Churches in Goa, the place also endorses exotic beaches.

Places of Interest:
  • Dona Paula Beach
  • Calangute Beach
  • Anjuna Beach
  • Colva Beach
  • Miramar Beach
  • Vagator Beach
  • Old Goa Churches
  • Shri Mangesh Temple

DELHI - Magnificent

Delhi, the capital city of India, boasts several monuments from the past and attracts tourists from all over the world. It is a land with a magnificent past and a perfect blend of Mughal and colonial architecture.

Places of Interest:
  • Red Fort
  • Jama Masjid
  • India Gate
  • Qutab Minar
  • Humayun’s Tomb
  • Lotus Temple

JAMMU & KASHMIR - Land Of High Passages

Set amidst the snow-capped Himalayas, Leh Ladakh has been the center of Buddhist culture since ancient times. It is a land of jagged peaks and alluring landscapes.

Places of Interest:
  • Shanti Stupa
  • Pangong Lake
  • Nubra Valley
  • Zanskar Valley
  • Hemis Monastery

HIMACHAL PRADESH - Destination For All Seasons

Shimla and Manali are popular hill stations in Himachal Pradesh, known for their scenic beauty, snow-capped mountains, and adventure activities.

Places of Interest:
  • Jakhoo Hill (Shimla)
  • Rohtang Pass (Manali)
  • Solang Valley
  • Hadimba Temple
  • Kufri

UTTAR PRADESH - Agra

Agra, home to the iconic Taj Mahal, is one of the most magnificent cities in India. It was once the capital of the Mughal Empire and is rich in history and architecture.

Places of Interest:
  • Taj Mahal
  • Agra Fort
  • Fatehpur Sikri
  • Itmad-ud-Daulah's Tomb

RAJASTHAN - Pink City

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the Pink City due to its pink sandstone buildings. It is famous for its forts, palaces, and vibrant culture.

Places of Interest:
  • Hawa Mahal
  • Amber Fort
  • City Palace
  • Jantar Mantar

KARNATAKA - Silicon Valley

Bangalore, the capital of Karnataka, is known as the Silicon Valley of India. It is a thriving modern city with a rich cultural heritage.

Places of Interest:
  • Lal Bagh
  • Cubbon Park
  • Bangalore Palace
  • Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace

KERALA - God’s Own Country

Kerala is known for its backwaters, beaches, and lush greenery. It is a popular tourist destination for its natural beauty and cultural heritage.

Places of Interest:
  • Alleppey Backwaters
  • Munnar Tea Gardens
  • Kovalam Beach
  • Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

TAMIL NADU - Ooty

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station in Tamil Nadu. It is famous for its tea plantations, scenic beauty, and pleasant climate.

Places of Interest:
  • Botanical Gardens
  • Doddabetta Peak
  • Ooty Lake
  • Rose Garden

UTTARAKHAND - Char Dham

Uttarakhand is home to the Char Dham pilgrimage sites - Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath. It is a spiritual and scenic destination.

Places of Interest:
  • Yamunotri Temple
  • Gangotri Temple
  • Kedarnath Temple
  • Badrinath Temple
